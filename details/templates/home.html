About

Aditya Fortune Towers is a 162 residential flat gated community which is located in Midhilapuri Vuda Colony, Madhurawada, Visakhapatnam. The complex consists of 3 residential apartment blocks each consisting of 54 three bed room spacious flats. The apartment complex includes a 6000sft club house, Swimming pool, two lifts per block, elegant landscaping and several other facilities. The developer for this project is Aditya Housing and Infrastructure Development Corporation (AHIDCO).
